Aphrodite, the Garden of the Perfumes
Aphr odite, the Garden of the Perfumes  (Spanish :
Afrodita, el Jardín de Los Perfumes ) is a 1998
Argentine  - Mali  drama film directed and written by
Argentine director Pablo César .
The film based on the Ancient Greek  god Aphrodite  is
an independent film production filmed in Mali . All of
the actors are from Mali  and only ever appea red in this
film. The film was produced by an Argentine
production team.
The story of Aphrodite, from her birth and her
relationships with the other gods of Greek mythology .
