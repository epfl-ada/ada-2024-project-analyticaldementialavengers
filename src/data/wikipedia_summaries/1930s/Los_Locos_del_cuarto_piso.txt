Los Locos del cuarto piso
Los Locos del cuar to piso is a black  and white 1937
Argentine  comedy film directed by Lisandro de la Tea
and writte n by Manuel Collazo , based on the play by
Francisco E. Collazo.[1] The film premiered on
November 10, 1937 in Buenos Aires .
