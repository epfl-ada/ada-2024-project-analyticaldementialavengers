A Million Bid (1927 film)
A Million  Bid is a 1927  silent  drama film directed by
Michael Curtiz  and starring Dolores Costello . It is
based on the 1908 play, Agnes , by Gladys Rankin
Drew writing under the pseudonym "Geor ge
Cameron".[1][2][3][4]
The story was previously filmed in 1914 under the
same name .
