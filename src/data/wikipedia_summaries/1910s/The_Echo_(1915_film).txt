The Echo (1915 film)

drama  directed by Tom Ricketts . The film stars Jack
Richardson , Vivian Rich , Charlotte Burton , B. Reeves
Eason , Perry Banks , Louise Lester , David Lythgoe ,
and Harry von Meter .
