Puppets of Fate (1933 film)
Puppets of Fate  is a 1933  British crime film direc ted
by Geor ge A. Cooper  and starring Godfrey Tearle , Isla
Bevan , Russell Thorndike , and Fred Grov es.[1] It was
shot at Twickenham Studios  in London  as a quota
quickie  for release by United Artists .[2] In the United
States  it was released under the title Wolves of the
Underworld .
