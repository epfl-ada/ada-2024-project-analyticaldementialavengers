Otello (1906 film)
Otello  is a 1906 Italian silent film based on the 1887
opera of the same name  by Giuseppe Verdi, both being
based on the William Shakespeare  play Othello .[1] The
film was directed  by and starred Mario Caserini ,
playing opposite his wife Maria Caserini . It is believed
to be the earliest film adaptation of the play, released in
Italy on 30 October 1906.
Gaston Velle is also credited as co-director .[2]
