72 Days
72 Days  (Serbo-Croatian : Sedamdeset i dva dana ) is a
2010 Croa tian-Serbian black comedy film direc ted by
Danilo Šerbedžija  and starring Rade Šerbedžija . The
film was selected as the Croatian entry for the Best
Foreign Language Film  at the 84th Academy
Awards ,[1][2] but it did not make the final shortlist.[3]
