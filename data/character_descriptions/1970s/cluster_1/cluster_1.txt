**Name:** Kaelin "Kae" Vex

**Age:** 25

**Background:** Kae grew up in a rough neighborhood with a history of gang violence. She lost her younger brother to gang warfare when she was just a teenager, which sparked her desire for revenge and justice.

**Physical Appearance:** Kae is lean and athletic, standing at 5'8" with short, spiky black hair and piercing green eyes. She has a scar above her left eyebrow from a childhood accident and a series of tattoos on her arms that symbolize her brother's memory.

**Occupation:** Kae works as a freelance journalist, investigating social justice issues and exposing corruption in the community. Her work often puts her at odds with powerful figures who seek to silence her.

**Personality:** Kae is driven, determined, and fiercely independent. She has a strong sense of justice and will stop at nothing to uncover the truth. However, her past traumas have left emotional scars, making her prone to anger and self-destructive tendencies.

**Daily Life:** Kae lives in a small studio apartment on the outskirts of the city, where she spends most of her time researching and writing. She is often seen sipping coffee at local cafes or walking the streets at night, gathering information and following leads.

**Motivations:** Kae's primary motivation is to seek revenge against those who killed her brother. However, as she delves deeper into her investigation, she begins to question whether justice is truly possible in a corrupt system. She becomes obsessed with exposing the truth, even if it means putting herself in harm's way.

**Conflict:** Kae struggles with her own demons, including PTSD and addiction. Her desire for revenge often clouds her judgment, leading her to take reckless risks that put her relationships and her life at risk.

**Quirks:** Kae has a habit of carrying a small notebook and pen with her everywhere she goes, jotting down observations and ideas as they strike her. She is also a voracious reader, devouring books on history, philosophy, and psychology to better understand the world around her.

Kae's character combines elements of Swan (The Warriors) - her drive for justice and survival instincts - Dom (The Vanishing) - her determination and desire for revenge - Alex (A Clockwork Orange) - her inner conflict and moral ambiguity - and Eric Draven (The Crow) - her loss and grief.
